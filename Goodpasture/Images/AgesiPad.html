Great Beginnings

Goodpasture Christian School is pleased to announce the opening of its Great Beginnings program in August of 2013. Classes begin for children at twelve months of age. Providing a safe, loving, and nurturing environment is our top priority. The Great Beginnings program is designed to grow each child physically, mentally, socially, emotionally, and spiritually.

Curriculum for Toddlers - Goodpasture has selected the High Reach curriculum for children in the one and two year old classes. In addition to this hands-on curriculum emphasizing interaction and assisted discovery, students are introduced to tech for tots classes utilizing iPads, music instruction with a certified instructor and tiny tots physical education classes. Children are taught as early as twelve moths of age that each cild is unique, special, and created by God.


Pre-K

Goodpasture's Pre-K programs provide a loving and caring environment in which students flourish with time for social interaction, group play, Bible lessons, the introduction of phonemic awareness skills, and mathematical concepts. We believe the love of learning begins at an early age and should be fostered each day.

All teachers in preschool are certified and licensed. They model a genuine love of children and share a relationship with Jesus Christ daily.


Kindergarten

Goodpasture Christian School Kindergarten is located in a building designed to resemble an old-fashioned schoolhouse, but is filled with state-of-the-art technology and advanced curriculum. In the "Little Red Schoolhouse" as it is affectionately known, students are taught a curriculum offering reading, mathematics, science, social studies, music, art, physical education, library, Spanish, and Mandarin Chinese! On top of that, iPad labs are utilized along with computer lab classes! With 100% reading success, Goodpasture's Kindergarten is truly a wonderful step towards your child's education


Elementary

Students in Goodpasture Christian Elementary School receive an outstanding education in an exceptional environment. Faculty and staff demonstrate their genuine love for students each day and provide a safe and loving atmosphere. In addition to the core subjects, students receive Spanish, Mandarin Chinese, technology lab, art, music, physical education, library, and STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) lab each week. Daily Bible lessons and weekly devotionals are also an important part of the elementary school experience.


Lower Middle School

Students in lower middle school are given their own wing in the fine arts building on campus. Daily class rotations include mathematics, reading and literature, science, science, and social studies. Bible classes are taught daily. Students in lower middle school also receive technology lab, music, art physical education, character development, and STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) lab each week. iPad carts are utilized for each grade level to assist with daily class work and projects.


Upper Middle School

Goodpasture's Upper Middle School consists of both 7th and 8th grades. Our goal is to provide opportunities to grow students spiritually, academically, socially, and athletically. Students have a Bible class and attend daily chapel. We run an alternating block schedule that consists of eight classes. Students have the opportunity to take honors or regular classes. Additionally, students have the chance to participate in an array of extracurricular activities. Finally, we also provide our students with various field trips and service projects to promote life-long service in the community.


High School

Goodpasture High School students will experience an education that promotes spirituality, academics, and extra-curricular activities. A student's high school career will be interwoven with a spiritual fabric that is unmatched. Daily devotionals, Bible classes, group devotionals, and other activities are enhanced by the spiritual leadership set forth by our caring faculty. Our teachers use this foundation to teach our students a variety of college preparatory classes that promote lifelong learning. Numerous Advanced Placement and Dual Enrollment courses enable our students to earn up to 45 hours of college credit prior to graduation! Overall, the Goodpasture High School experience is one that enables a student to grow in all aspects of their lives. It prepares young men and women for their college future and gives them the tools to be successful in years to come.
